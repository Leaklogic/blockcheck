About LeakLogic UK

LeakLogic is focused on one diagnosis problem space: confirming whether combustion gases are getting into the cooling system. The aim is simple: give UK buyers and mechanics a clear, practical way to confirm a suspected blown head gasket before they commit to teardown, repair negotiation, or further testing.

The current Block-Check Blue kit is designed as a universal head gasket tester for petrol, diesel, and LPG engines, and it is supported by downloadable manuals, result guidance, and product help on this site.

What we focus on

Combustion leak testing, head gasket diagnosis, result interpretation, fitment help, and buyer guidance for UK vehicle owners and workshops.

Who the kit is built for

DIY owners, used-car buyers, and garages that need a straightforward combustion leak detector with clear instructions and practical support.
