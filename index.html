Frequently asked

Does it work on diesels?

Yes. Block‑Check Blue™ detects combustion CO₂ in any liquid‑cooled petrol, diesel or LPG engine.

How many tests in one bottle?

About 15 – you only need 2 mL per test.

How long does the fluid last?

18 months sealed. Store the bottle cool, dark and tightly capped.

Is it safe to pour down the drain?

Yes. The spent solution is mildly alkaline and non‑toxic; small workshop quantities can be flushed with plenty of water.
