Quick setup checklist

  • Start with a cold engine and safe working area
  • Check that the bung or adapter seals properly
  • Use clean test fluid at the correct level
  • Warm the engine before judging the result
  • Keep coolant out of the test chambers

Troubleshooting

  • No bubbling: check seal and engine temperature
  • Messy fluid: empty, clean, and retest with fresh fluid
  • Weak colour change: repeat with a cleaner setup
  • Hard-to-fit tank: read the PDF and reposition the bung
