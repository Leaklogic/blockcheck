How it works

1) Draw gases through fluid

Insert the tester into the expansion tank and let the engine idle. Exhaust gases (if present) bubble through the blue fluid.

2) Watch the colour

Blue stays blue = no leak. Blue turning green/yellow = combustion gases detected.

3) Decide with confidence

Get a definitive yes/no in under 2 minutes so you can repair, negotiate, or rule out a head gasket issue.
